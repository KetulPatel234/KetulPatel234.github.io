HISTORY

The temple, at a height of 1,585 metres (5,200 ft) is 12 km from Katra on Trikuta hill. It is about 61 km from Jammu city.A geological study of the Holy Cave has indicated its age to be nearly a million years. There is also a mention of the Trikuta hill in Rigveda, the place where the temple is located. The Mahabharata, which gives the account of the Pandavas and the Kurukshetra War, does mention the worship of goddess Vaishno Devi. Before the Kurukshetra War Arjuna is said to have worshipped Durga by the advice of Lord Krishna for the blessings. Pleased by his devotion, Mother Goddess appeared in front of him in the form of Vaishno Devi. When goddess appeared, Arjuna started praising her with a stotra

TOURIST ATTRACTIONS

Vaishno Devi shrine is located in the lap of Trikuta hills which is 13 Kms from base camp Katra, which is one of the prominent town of District Reasi and 63 kms from Jammu city.The Shrine is famous pilgrimage centre in Jammu and Kashmir state. It is managed by Shri Mata Vaishno Devi Shrine Board since 1986. Million of Pilgrims from within and outside the country visit this holy place every year.

TIMING

The temple gates usually open before sunrise, often around 4:00 AM or 5:00 AM. Darshan (worship) is allowed throughout the day. Aarti (rituals) are performed at specific times during the day. The temple gates typically close in the late evening, around 9:00 PM or 10:00 PM.

TRANSPORT GUIDE

How to Get There

By Road

The nearest bus station to the Vaishno Devi Temple is the Katra Bus Stand. Katra is the base camp for pilgrims visiting the temple, and the bus stand serves as a major transportation hub for travelers arriving by bus

By Train

The nearest railway station to the Vaishno Devi Temple is the Shri Mata Vaishno Devi Katra Railway Station. It is located in Katra, Jammu and Kashmir, and serves as the primary railhead for pilgrims visiting the temple. The station is situated at a distance of approximately 20 kilometers from the base camp of the temple

By Air

The nearest airport to the Vaishno Devi Temple is the Jammu Airport. The Jammu Airport is situated approximately 50 kilometers away from Katra
