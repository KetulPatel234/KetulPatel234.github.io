Kashmir
Explore the scenic beauty of Kashmir, a true paradise on Earth. From majestic mountains to serene lakes, Kashmir offers an unforgettable escape into nature's embrace. Indulge in houseboat stays, trekking adventures, and delightful cuisine, all amidst the warm hospitality of its people.
Explore famous places in Kashmir
Vaishnodevi

Nestled amidst the picturesque Trikuta Mountains in Kashmir, the Vaishno Devi Temple stands as a beacon of devotion and spirituality. Perched at an altitude of 5,200 feet, this sacred pilgrimage site draws millions of devotees each year, seeking blessings from the divine Mother Goddess, Vaishno Devi. Embark on a spiritual journey through the winding mountain trails, adorned with breathtaking vistas and serene natural beauty, to reach the holy cave shrine. Here, amidst the tranquil surroundings, devotees offer prayers and seek divine guidance, experiencing a profound sense of peace and fulfillment. The Vaishno Devi Temple in Kashmir is not just a place of worship but a spiritual sanctuary that touches the hearts of all who visit.

Gulmarg

Nestled amid the majestic peaks of the Pir Panjal range in Kashmir, Gulmarg emerges as a haven of natural splendor and outdoor adventure. Situated at an altitude of 8,694 feet, this enchanting hill station attracts travelers year-round with its pristine beauty and exhilarating recreational activities. Embark on a journey through lush meadows and towering pine forests, where every turn reveals breathtaking vistas of snow-capped mountains and verdant valleys. Whether skiing down powdery slopes in winter or trekking amidst blooming wildflowers in summer, Gulmarg offers an immersive experience in nature's embrace. Amidst this serene landscape, visitors find solace and rejuvenation, making Gulmarg not just a destination but a soul-stirring sanctuary for all who venture here.

Sonmarg

Nestled amidst the rugged peaks of the Himalayas in Kashmir, Sonmarg beckons travelers with its awe-inspiring beauty and tranquil ambiance. Situated at an altitude of 9,000 feet, this scenic destination is renowned for its pristine meadows, glistening glaciers, and meandering rivers. Embark on a journey through winding mountain roads, where each turn unveils breathtaking vistas of snow-clad peaks and lush green valleys. Whether trekking along ancient trails, indulging in adventurous activities like river rafting, or simply basking in the serenity of its alpine landscape, Sonmarg offers an unforgettable escape into nature's embrace. Amidst this idyllic setting, visitors find solace and inspiration, making Sonmarg not just a destination but a soulful sanctuary for all who seek its beauty.
