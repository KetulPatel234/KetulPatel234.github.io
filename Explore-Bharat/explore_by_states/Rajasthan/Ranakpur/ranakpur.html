Rankpur Jain Temple

HISTORY

Ranakpur Jain temple or Chaturmukha Dharana Vihara is a Śvētāmbara Jain temple at Ranakpur dedicated to Tirthankara Rishabhanatha.The temple is located in a village of Ranakpur near Sadri town in the Pali district of Rajasthan. Darna Shah, a local Jain businessperson, started construction of the temple in the 15th century following a divine vision. The temple honours Adinath, the first Tirthankar of the present half-cycle (avasarpiṇī) according to Jain cosmology. The Ranakpur temple is one of the largest and most important temples of Jain culture. The campus includes various temples such as Chaumukha temple, Surya temple, Suparshvanatha temple and Amba temple.

TOURIST ATTRACTIONS

The massive temple structure, raised entirely in softly colored marble, stands atop a base of subterranean vaults, sprawling in an area of over 48,000 square feet. Entering into the temple complex, it is difficult for someone to not be mesmerized by the vivacity and scale of its designs. The moment you step into Ranakpur Temple is the very moment you begin unraveling its veritable splendor; rays of light steal through carved pillar-corridor, emanating their warmth into the cups of ornate domes overhead. Encompassed by this temple complex are the Parshavanath Temple, Chaumukha Temple, Surya Temple and the Amba Mata Temple. The Chaumukha Temple is the principal temple that houses the main deity, Lord Adinath, the first Jain Tirthankara.

TIMINGS

The Ranakpur Jain Temple is generally open to visitors every day of the week, including weekends and public holidays. typically the temple is open from early morning until late afternoon or early evening. It's recommended to visit during daylight hours to fully appreciate the intricate architecture and the serene surroundings.

How to Get There

By Road

The nearest bus station to the Ranakpur Jain Temple is the Ranakpur Bus Stand. This bus stand is located in close proximity to the temple complex, making it convenient for visitors to access the temple via public transportation.

By Train

The nearest railway station to Ranakpur is the Falna Railway Station, which is situated approximately 30 kilometers away. From Falna Railway Station, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Ranakpur Jain Temple.

By Air

The nearest airport to Ranakpur is Maharana Pratap Airport, also known as Udaipur Airport (IATA: UDR). It is located in Udaipur, Rajasthan, approximately 95 kilometers away from Ranakpur. From Udaipur Airport, you can hire a taxi or use other transportation options to reach Ranakpur, which usually takes around 2 to 3 hours by road, depending on traffic conditions.
